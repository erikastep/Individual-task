CineNook Membership

CineNook is a members-only film club based in Vilnius, Lithuania. Standard annual membership costs 45 EUR per year. Students pay a reduced rate of 25 EUR per year with a valid student ID.

Membership includes free entry to all Tuesday screenings and a 20% discount at the CineNook cafe. Members can bring one guest per month for free.

Membership cards are issued within 7 days of sign-up and must be renewed every January.
