CineNook Events

CineNook hosts three regular events each week. Tuesday nights are Classic Nights, showing restored films made before 1980. Thursday nights are Director's Focus, dedicated to a single filmmaker each month. Saturday afternoons are Family Matinees, suitable for all ages.

The annual CineNook Short Film Award is held every October. The 2025 winner was a short film called "Paper Boats" by director Ruta Kaz. The prize is 2000 EUR and a one-year distribution deal.
