CineNook FAQ

Can I watch films online? No. CineNook screenings are in-person only, at the Old Town venue on Pilies Street.

Is food allowed in the screening room? Outside food is not allowed, but drinks purchased at the CineNook cafe may be brought in.

What is the seating capacity? The main screening room seats 60 people. Booking ahead is recommended for Thursday Director's Focus nights, which often sell out.

How do I contact CineNook? Email hello@cinenook.example or visit the front desk during opening hours, which are 4pm to 10pm on screening days.
